Research Study Information

Study Purpose: This experiment is designed to evaluate educational programming tools and their effectiveness in teaching computational thinking skills.

What You'll Do: You will complete a series of programming challenges using visual block-based coding to control a robot in an isometric environment.

Data Collection: We will collect interaction data, completion times, and solution approaches. All data is anonymous and identified only by a randomly generated ID.

Time Commitment: The study typically takes 20-30 minutes to complete.
